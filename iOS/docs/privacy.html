Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Filtercycle ("the App") is developed by Shimon Deitel. This policy explains what data the App collects and how it is used.

Data We Collect

Filtercycle stores the filters you add (name, category, replacement interval, last-changed date) and your change-log history entirely on your device. This data is not transmitted to us or any third party, and we do not operate any backend server that receives it.

Notifications

If you grant permission, the App may schedule a single local reminder notification after you reach your free monthly logging limit. This notification is scheduled and delivered entirely on-device via Apple's notification framework; we do not receive any data as a result.

Purchases

Subscription purchases are processed by Apple via StoreKit. We do not receive or store your payment information. Apple's own privacy policy governs data collected during purchase and subscription management.

Analytics and Advertising

Filtercycle does not include third-party analytics or advertising SDKs.

Data Deletion

Deleting the App from your device removes all locally stored filter and log data. There is no server-side account or copy of your data to separately delete.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent to the developer via the App Store support contact listed on the Filtercycle App Store page.
